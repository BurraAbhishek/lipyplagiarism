How to plagiarize research papers

Writing a paper is hard, and you just had a great idea.

Instead of working on your research, why not just copy and paste content from other papers? We can complete the paper very quickly, and get away with the credits because no one will notice.

Unfortunately, a lot of people have the same idea everyday, and it is robbing others of their hard-earned innovative idea and research work.

Please do not plagiarize other papers. You get nothing out of it, and make innovation and research a worse experience for everyone, yourself included. Doing so will only invite legal action against you.

Instead, why not do all the hard work? It will take longer but be much more rewarding.
